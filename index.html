About

Anthony Pompliano is an entrepreneur and investor known for delivering outsized returns for investors and shareholders. He is the Founder and CEO of Professional Capital Management (PCM), where he invests across public and private markets and incubates industry-leading companies in media, real estate, personal finance, and events. He has launched and exited numerous businesses and invested in more than 300 private companies.

Before founding PCM, Mr. Pompliano led product and growth teams at Facebook. He previously served as an Infantry Sergeant in the U.S. Army and was deployed to Iraq from 2008 to 2009. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from Bucknell University.
