About

Anthony Pompliano is an entrepreneur and investor with a track record of creating outsized returns for investors and shareholders.

As Founder and Chief Executive Officer of investment firm Professional Capital Management, Anthony invests across public and private markets, including the incubation of several industry-leading companies across media, real estate, personal finance, and events. He has successfully launched and exited numerous businesses and invested in more than 300 private companies. He began his career at Facebook, where he led product and growth teams. Before entering the private sector, he served in the U.S. Army as an Infantry Sergeant and was deployed to Iraq from 2008 to 2009.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Bucknell University.
